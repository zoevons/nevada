About Us

We are situated in the heart of the Nevada Desert and offer some fantastic horse riding trails. Open all year round, our guides will show you some stunning scenery on one of our beautiful horses.

We offer a variety of different trails, most are suitable for all ages and all levels of riders. We also offer sunrise and sunset trails across the beautiful Nevada Desert. All our guides are experienced riders and have some great knowledge about our trail locations which will ensure you have an amazing time.
